Submission Guidelines:

  • We welcome submissions from everyone except current teaching faculty at the University of Colorado Boulder.
  • We only publish creative nonfiction writing, fact-checking as necessary.
  • Any necessary textual citations should follow Chicago Manual of Style footnote format.
  • AI-generated submissions will be rejected immediately.
  • Hindsight published all forms of creative nonfiction (including poetry and creative scholarship or journalism, etc.). Pieces for our Changing Skies issues must address or center around climate issues.
  • All submissions are considered for publication in print, online, or both.
  • We only accept the following file types:
    • Word files (.docx, .doc) for writing.
    • JPG’s or PDFs for art or work including graphics.
  • For all prose, use double-spaced Times New Roman 12 point type, 1” margins, and indented paragraphs.
  • For prose in sections, indicate space breaks with a centered hashtag.
  • For all poetry, use single-spaced Times New Roman 12 point type, double spacing between stanzas.
  • Please remove your name from your piece, including the file name, first page with title, headers, and pagination, to ensure anonymous reviewing. If your writing includes an identifiable name for you (beyond simply a common first name, etc.), please replace that with “MY NAME” and we will restore your name in editing if accepting your piece for publication.
